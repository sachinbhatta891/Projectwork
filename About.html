About Our College

Vinayak Siddha College is a premier institution dedicated to "academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development". Our mission is to provide "high-quality education" and create future leaders who excel in their respective fields.Vinayak Siddha College, since its establishment in the year 2013, has been one of the prestigious institutions in the prime location of Chabahil and has been making a prior effort for the excellent academic environment. Realizing the prior challenges and short comings in private sector, VSC always stood for the sake of students and helped students grow personally and professionally offering holistic and quality education. The academic results have proven that the VSC has assured ECAs, CCAs, social awareness programs, cultural programs, students welfare programs, and instill right skills and attitude in the students. A highly academic standard is a priority for our institution and it is being achieved through a body of well trained and highly qualified faculties. VSC offers a true learning environment nurtured by the latest innovation and technology.We also offer excellent education system, quality learning exposure, and technocratic teaching learning practices.

Ethical Gudielines

# After being admitted in VSC as a student, he/she must follow all the rules and regulations mentioned in the VSC Code of Conduct. If anyone is found violating the prescribed code of conduct, the management committee and administration of the academy have the right to cancel his/herregistration.

# If a student is found being irregular or absent in college (while conducting regular class or internal exam)without prior notice and permission for more than 3 days, the management committee and college administration deserve the right to cancel his/her registration.

# If any due fees of the college have not been cleared within 60 days of the last notice, the nameof the student from college will be cancelled.

# Health of the student must be sound. If health (physically and mentally) is not proper, he/she willnot be allowed to pursue education in the academy.

# If students are found wearing the extra clothes or out dresses except "The fixed and defined dresscode of the academy", he/she will not be allowed to attend the class. The boys must keep their hair short and the girls must tie-up their hair. Any sort of jewelries and makeups are strictlyprohibited.

# Students are not allowed to bring any type of electronic gadgets. If those gadgets are found withthe students in the college premises, they will be seized forever. Cards, personal diaries, autographs,and gifts are also not allowed within the college premises.

# The college management has the ultimate rights to expel the students; therefore, we would notconsider any misbehavior with teachers, administrative staffs, and managerial members.

# Paid amount will not be refundable incaseof discontinuity of the study after being admitted in VSCollege.
